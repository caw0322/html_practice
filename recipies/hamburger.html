Hamburger!

This hamburger is meaty and tasty!

Ingredients you will need

  • ground beef
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • bun
  • your favorite sliced cheese
  • onions

Steps!

  1. shape the ground beef into patties
  2. On high heat cook the burger for 3 minutes on one side
  3. Flip the burger
  4. cook the burger for 5 minutes
  5. place cheese on burger and allow to cook for 1 minute
  6. remove from heat place patty on bun
  7. cut onions and tomator into slices
  8. place onions and tomato on burger
